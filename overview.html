About This Website

Healthy Habits Navigator is an educational tool designed to help young adults understand how lifestyle factors are discussed in early-onset colorectal cancer research.

Important Disclaimer

This tool is educational only.

It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or cancer risk prediction.

If you have personal health concerns, a licensed healthcare professional can provide guidance tailored to your situation.
